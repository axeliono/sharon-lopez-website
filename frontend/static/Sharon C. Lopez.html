About Sharon C. Lopez

Sharon is a licensed professional counselor, author, speaker, and hope-giver. She earned her Masters of Science in Counseling Psychology from Southwestern Assemblies of God University in 2011 and has compassionately assisted children, adults, and families in crisis and transition. Prior to this work, Sharon married her high school sweetheart, Mark, and earned her Bachelor of Science in Church Ministries. She and Mark served as youth pastors, worship pastors, and executive pastors in Maryland, New Mexico, and Texas. Together they have four amazing children, Madison, Miquela, Elisha, Elise, and a son-in-love, Chandler. Sharon views individuals as Gods greatest treasure. She is a Jesus-follower, life-speaker, and disciple-maker.

Sharons Vision

You can live in the BONUS

You will often hear Sharon stating she is living in the bonus! She is unashamed in her testimony of Gods faithfulness and goodness in every season. In fact, she believes HE is trustworthy in the big, small, hard, easy - through seasons of feasting or fasting. While there are hard moments in Sharons hope-journey, such as sexual assault in 2009 and breast cancer in 2023, she is resilient in her faith and in THE ONE who holds her. She learned that God is more than faithful! He can be trusted in ALL! She chooses to speak life and hope knowing that life on earth is a vapor (James 4:14), and there is so much more to this journey! She is looking for the blessed hope and glorious appearing of our Great God and Savior Jesus Christ (Titus 2:13).
